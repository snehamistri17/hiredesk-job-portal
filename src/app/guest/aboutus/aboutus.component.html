Our Journey

From a Dorm Room to
India's #1 Job Platform

It started in 2020 when two engineers, frustrated by outdated recruitment processes, decided to build something better. HireDesk began as a small campus placement portal serving 3 colleges in Ahmedabad.

Within a year, over 200 companies were using HireDesk to post jobs and discover talent. Today, we serve millions of job seekers across India, with headquarters in Ahmedabad and offices in Bangalore, Mumbai, and Delhi.

We've helped place over 500,000 professionals in roles they love — and we're just getting started.
